I generate various polygenic scores (PGSs) for different datasets, including Educational Attainment, Mental Health, Risk Tolerance, Physical Health, Height, and Weight, with more than 50 PGSs available. These scores are constructed using summary statistics from top genetics journals, such as Nature Genetics, for each dataset listed below.

Additionally, I have built a server on Cambridge’s High-Performance Computing (HPC) system to generate PGSs with valid summary statistics input. I am open to collaborations with economics researchers who are interested in developing high-quality PGSs tailored to their research needs.
